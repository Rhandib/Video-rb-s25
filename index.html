What is Yodeling

Yodeling is a form of singing that involves rapid and repeated changes of pitch between the low-pitch chest register and the high-pitch head register. It's traditionally used by herders in the mountains to communicate across vast distances — and now, it’s a global sensation with roots in folk, country, and even modern pop.
